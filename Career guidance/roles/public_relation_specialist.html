Public Relations Specialist

Job Description

A Public Relations Specialist manages an organization’s public image and communications with the media, customers, investors, and the general public. They help build and maintain a positive reputation through strategic messaging, media coverage, and community engagement.

Skills Required

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Strong interpersonal and networking abilities
  • Media relations and crisis communication
  • Creativity and storytelling
  • Strategic thinking and brand awareness
  • Social media proficiency

Educational Requirements

A bachelor’s degree in Public Relations, Communications, Journalism, Marketing, or a related field is typically required. Advanced certifications or a master’s in communications can provide additional opportunities.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Developing PR strategies and campaigns
  • Writing press releases, speeches, and media kits
  • Coordinating interviews and press conferences
  • Managing crisis communications
  • Monitoring media coverage and public sentiment
  • Building relationships with journalists and influencers
  • Handling internal and external communications

Average Salary

₹4,00,000 to ₹10,00,000 per year (India, based on experience and company).

Companies Offering This Role

  • Adfactors PR
  • Perfect Relations
  • Edelman India
  • Genesis BCW
  • Weber Shandwick
  • Corporate communications departments of major companies (e.g., Tata, Reliance, Infosys)
