Construction Project Manager

Job Description

A Construction Project Manager oversees the planning, coordination, budgeting, and supervision of construction projects from start to finish. They ensure projects are completed on time, within scope, and within budget while maintaining safety and quality standards.

Skills Required

  • Strong leadership and communication skills
  • Project management and organizational abilities
  • Knowledge of construction methods and materials
  • Budgeting and financial planning
  • Problem-solving and conflict resolution
  • Proficiency with project management software

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering, Construction Management, Architecture, or a related field is typically required. Professional certifications such as PMP or certification in construction management are advantageous.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Planning and defining project scope, goals, and deliverables
  • Coordinating with architects, engineers, and contractors
  • Managing project timelines, resources, and budget
  • Ensuring compliance with building codes and safety regulations
  • Supervising site operations and resolving issues
  • Communicating with stakeholders and providing progress updates

Average Salary

₹6,00,000 to ₹20,00,000 per year (India, depending on experience and project size).
