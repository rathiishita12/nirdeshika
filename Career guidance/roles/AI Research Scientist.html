AI Research Scientist

Job Description

AI Research Scientists explore cutting-edge artificial intelligence technologies and develop innovative algorithms and models. They focus on advancing the field through theoretical work, experimentation, and application of AI to solve real-world problems.

Skills Required

  • Strong knowledge of machine learning and deep learning algorithms
  • Proficiency in programming languages like Python, R, or C++
  • Research and analytical skills
  • Mathematical and statistical proficiency
  • Experience with AI frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, etc.
  • Problem-solving and critical thinking abilities

Educational Requirements

A Ph.D. in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, or related disciplines is preferred. Strong academic research background and published papers in peer-reviewed journals are advantageous.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Conducting advanced AI research and experiments
  • Publishing findings in academic journals and conferences
  • Developing novel algorithms and improving existing models
  • Collaborating with engineering and product teams to implement solutions
  • Staying updated with advancements in AI technologies
  • Contributing to open-source projects and initiatives

Average Salary

₹10,00,000 to ₹35,00,000 per year (India, varies with expertise and organization).

Companies Offering This Role

  • Google DeepMind
  • Microsoft Research
  • Amazon AI
  • IBM Research
  • OpenAI
  • Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) and AI Startups
