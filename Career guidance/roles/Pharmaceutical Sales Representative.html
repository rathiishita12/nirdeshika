Pharmaceutical Sales Representative

Job Description

A Pharmaceutical Sales Representative promotes and sells their company's pharmaceutical products to healthcare professionals. They build relationships with doctors, pharmacists, and medical staff to increase product awareness and drive sales.

Skills Required

  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills
  • Sales and negotiation abilities
  • Knowledge of medical and pharmaceutical products
  • Time management and organizational skills
  • Presentation and public speaking abilities
  • Customer-focused mindset

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in pharmacy, life sciences, business, or a related field is typically required. Additional training in pharmaceutical regulations and product knowledge is often provided by the employer.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Visiting healthcare professionals to promote pharmaceutical products
  • Providing product information and scientific data
  • Achieving sales targets and expanding client base
  • Attending medical conferences and industry events
  • Maintaining records of sales and client interactions
  • Keeping up to date with new products and market trends

Average Salary

₹3,00,000 to ₹8,00,000 per year (India, varies with performance and incentives).
