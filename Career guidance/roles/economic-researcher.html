Economic Researcher

Job Description

An Economic Researcher analyzes economic data to understand trends, inform policy decisions, and provide insights for businesses, governments, and academic institutions. They use models and statistics to interpret complex economic phenomena.

Skills Required

  • Strong analytical and quantitative skills
  • Proficiency in statistical tools and software (e.g., R, Stata, Python)
  • Economic modeling and forecasting
  • Research writing and data interpretation
  • Attention to detail and logical reasoning
  • Communication of technical findings to non-technical audiences

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in Economics or related field is required. A master’s or PhD is often necessary for advanced roles, especially in academia or policy institutions.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Conducting in-depth economic research and analysis
  • Creating economic models and forecasts
  • Analyzing policy impacts and market trends
  • Publishing findings in reports and academic journals
  • Providing recommendations to policymakers and stakeholders
  • Presenting data-driven insights

Average Salary

₹5,00,000 to ₹12,00,000 per year (India, based on experience and sector).

Companies Offering This Role

  • Reserve Bank of India
  • NITI Aayog
  • Ministry of Finance
  • World Bank & IMF (India programs)
  • Private think tanks and research organizations (e.g., NCAER, CMIE)
  • Top consulting and analytics firms
