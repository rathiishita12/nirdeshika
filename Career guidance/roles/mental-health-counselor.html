Mental Health Counselor

Job Description

Mental Health Counselors help individuals manage and overcome mental and emotional issues. They provide therapy, develop treatment plans, and support clients dealing with stress, depression, anxiety, and relationship issues.

Skills Required

  • Empathy and strong listening skills
  • Emotional intelligence and patience
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Problem-solving and decision-making ability
  • Knowledge of mental health disorders and therapies
  • Confidentiality and ethical judgment

Educational Requirements

A master’s degree in Psychology, Counseling, or Social Work is generally required. Additional certifications and clinical training are necessary for licensure and professional practice.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Conducting individual or group counseling sessions
  • Developing personalized treatment plans
  • Monitoring client progress and adjusting therapies
  • Providing crisis intervention when necessary
  • Maintaining accurate and confidential records
  • Collaborating with other mental health professionals

Average Salary

₹3,00,000 to ₹7,00,000 per year (India, based on experience and work setting).

Organizations Offering This Role

  • Hospitals and mental health clinics
  • Educational institutions
  • Rehabilitation centers
  • NGOs and counseling centers
  • Private practice
