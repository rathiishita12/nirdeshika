Teacher (Secondary Education)

Job Description

A Secondary Education Teacher is responsible for educating students typically in grades 6 through 12. They develop lesson plans, deliver lectures, evaluate student performance, and foster a positive and engaging classroom environment.

Skills Required

  • Strong communication and presentation skills
  • Classroom management abilities
  • Expertise in subject matter (e.g., Math, Science, English)
  • Patience, empathy, and adaptability
  • Assessment and evaluation skills
  • Use of educational technology and tools

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in Education (B.Ed.) or a subject-specific degree with a teaching certification is required. A master's degree may be preferred for certain subjects or higher grades.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Developing and implementing lesson plans
  • Delivering subject content in engaging ways
  • Assessing and evaluating student progress
  • Maintaining discipline and a productive classroom environment
  • Collaborating with parents and school staff
  • Participating in school activities and development programs

Average Salary

₹2,50,000 to ₹6,00,000 per year (India, depending on school, location, and experience).

Organizations Offering This Role

  • Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS)
  • Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNV)
  • Private CBSE/ICSE/State Board Schools
  • International Schools
  • Online education platforms like BYJU’S, Vedantu
