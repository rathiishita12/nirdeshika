Robotics Engineer

Job Description

A Robotics Engineer designs, develops, and maintains robotic systems used in industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, defense, and space exploration. They integrate mechanics, electronics, and computer science to create autonomous and semi-autonomous machines.

Skills Required

  • Strong programming and software development skills (C/C++, Python, ROS)
  • Knowledge of mechanics, electronics, and control systems
  • Problem-solving and critical thinking
  • 3D modeling and CAD software proficiency
  • Understanding of AI and machine learning algorithms
  • Team collaboration and communication

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in Robotics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or a related field is required. A master’s or Ph.D. is often preferred for advanced roles.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Designing and prototyping robotic systems
  • Developing and testing software for automation
  • Collaborating with cross-functional teams
  • Conducting simulations and performance analysis
  • Ensuring safety and efficiency in robotic operations
  • Documenting technical specifications and improvements

Average Salary

₹5,00,000 to ₹15,00,000 per year (India, varies by industry and expertise).
