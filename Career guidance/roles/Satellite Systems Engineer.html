Satellite Systems Engineer

Job Description

A Satellite Systems Engineer designs, develops, tests, and maintains satellite systems and components. This role involves working on both ground-based and space-based systems, ensuring communication, navigation, and data transmission work flawlessly.

Skills Required

  • Strong background in aerospace or electrical engineering
  • Knowledge of satellite communication and navigation systems
  • Understanding of orbital mechanics and space environment
  • Proficiency in CAD software and simulation tools
  • Problem-solving and analytical thinking
  • Team collaboration and project management skills

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in Aerospace Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or a related field is required. A master's degree or relevant certifications in satellite systems engineering can enhance career prospects.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Designing satellite systems and subsystems
  • Collaborating on system integration and testing
  • Conducting performance analysis and simulations
  • Monitoring satellite operations and troubleshooting issues
  • Ensuring compliance with aerospace safety standards
  • Preparing technical documentation and reports

Average Salary

₹6,00,000 to ₹18,00,000 per year (India, depending on experience and specialization).

Organizations Offering This Role

  • Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)
  • Defense Research and Development Organization (DRDO)
  • Private aerospace firms like Antrix, Pixxel, Skyroot Aerospace
  • Satellite communication companies
  • International space agencies and contractors
