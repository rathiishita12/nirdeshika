Psychologist

Job Description

A Psychologist studies mental processes and human behavior by observing, interpreting, and recording how people relate to one another and to their environments. They help clients cope with mental health challenges and improve well-being.

Skills Required

  • Empathy and active listening
  • Strong analytical and observational skills
  • Knowledge of psychological theories and practices
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Patience and emotional resilience
  • Ethical judgment and confidentiality

Educational Requirements

A master's degree or doctoral degree (Ph.D. or Psy.D.) in Psychology is typically required. Licensure or certification is also mandatory for clinical practice.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Conducting psychological assessments and evaluations
  • Diagnosing and treating mental and emotional disorders
  • Providing therapy and counseling to individuals or groups
  • Developing treatment plans and monitoring progress
  • Conducting research to improve understanding of behavior
  • Collaborating with other healthcare professionals

Average Salary

₹3,00,000 to ₹10,00,000 per year (India, varies by specialization and experience).

Organizations Offering This Role

  • Hospitals and mental health clinics
  • Educational institutions and universities
  • Private counseling practices
  • Corporate wellness programs
  • Government and military organizations
  • Research and academic institutions
