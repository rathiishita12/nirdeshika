Project Manager

Job Description

A Project Manager is responsible for planning, executing, and closing projects according to deadlines and within budget. This role involves coordinating team members, managing resources, and communicating with stakeholders to ensure successful project delivery.

Skills Required

  • Strong organizational and leadership skills
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities
  • Time management and multitasking
  • Proficiency in project management tools (e.g., Jira, Trello, MS Project)
  • Problem-solving and decision-making skills
  • Risk management and budgeting knowledge

Educational Requirements

A bachelor’s degree in Business, Engineering, or a related field is typically required. A Project Management Professional (PMP) certification or equivalent is highly valued.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Defining project scope, goals, and deliverables
  • Creating project plans and timelines
  • Coordinating internal teams and resources
  • Managing budgets and tracking expenditures
  • Identifying and mitigating risks
  • Monitoring progress and adjusting plans as needed

Average Salary

₹8,00,000 to ₹20,00,000 per year (India, varies by experience and industry).
