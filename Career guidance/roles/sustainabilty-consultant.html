Sustainability Consultant

Job Description

A Sustainability Consultant advises organizations on strategies to reduce environmental impact, improve energy efficiency, and implement sustainable practices. They evaluate existing processes and provide recommendations to achieve environmental and regulatory goals.

Skills Required

  • Knowledge of environmental science and sustainability practices
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Project management and planning
  • Effective communication and presentation abilities
  • Regulatory compliance understanding
  • Data analysis and reporting skills

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's or master’s degree in Environmental Science, Sustainability, Engineering, or a related field is required. Certifications like LEED or ISO 14001 can be advantageous.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Assessing environmental impact and sustainability performance
  • Developing and implementing sustainability strategies
  • Advising on regulatory compliance and green certification
  • Conducting audits and creating reports
  • Collaborating with clients and stakeholders
  • Providing training on sustainability practices

Average Salary

₹6,00,000 to ₹15,00,000 per year (India, depending on experience and firm).

Companies Offering This Role

  • KPMG Sustainability Services
  • PwC India
  • Tata Sustainability Group
  • Environmental NGOs
  • Green building consultancies
