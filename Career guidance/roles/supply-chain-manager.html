Supply Chain Manager

Job Description

A Supply Chain Manager oversees the entire supply chain operations, including procurement, logistics, and inventory management. Their goal is to streamline operations, reduce costs, and ensure timely delivery of products and services.

Skills Required

  • Strategic planning and problem-solving
  • Inventory and logistics management
  • Strong analytical and organizational skills
  • Proficiency in supply chain software (e.g., SAP, Oracle)
  • Excellent communication and negotiation abilities
  • Leadership and team coordination

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in Supply Chain Management, Logistics, Business Administration, or a related field is typically required. A master's degree or certifications like CSCP or CPIM are advantageous.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Planning and overseeing supply chain strategy
  • Managing procurement and sourcing activities
  • Supervising warehousing and inventory management
  • Coordinating logistics and transportation
  • Analyzing supply chain data and performance
  • Collaborating with suppliers and internal departments

Average Salary

₹6,00,000 to ₹15,00,000 per year (India, depending on experience and industry).
