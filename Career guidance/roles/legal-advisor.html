Legal Advisor

Job Description

A Legal Advisor provides legal guidance and support to organizations or individuals, ensuring that their operations comply with applicable laws and regulations. They may represent their clients in legal matters and help draft and review legal documents.

Skills Required

  • Strong knowledge of laws and legal procedures
  • Excellent analytical and research skills
  • Effective communication and negotiation
  • Attention to detail and logical reasoning
  • Document drafting and legal interpretation
  • Dispute resolution and advocacy skills

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in Law (LLB) is required. Further specialization (LLM) or certification in corporate or constitutional law can enhance opportunities.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Advising clients on legal rights and obligations
  • Drafting and reviewing contracts, agreements, and legal documents
  • Ensuring legal compliance of company operations
  • Representing clients in legal proceedings when necessary
  • Conducting legal research and staying updated on laws
  • Assisting in dispute resolution and negotiation

Average Salary

₹5,00,000 to ₹15,00,000 per year (India, depending on experience and field of specialization).

Companies Offering This Role

  • AZB & Partners
  • Cyril Amarchand Mangaldas
  • Luthra & Luthra Law Offices
  • Corporate legal departments (e.g., Infosys, Wipro, Tata Group)
  • Government agencies and public sector units
  • Independent law firms and consultancy agencies
