Healthcare Data Analyst

Job Description

A Healthcare Data Analyst interprets medical data to help healthcare organizations make informed decisions. They work with electronic health records (EHR), patient satisfaction surveys, and operational data to improve quality of care and reduce costs.

Skills Required

  • Proficiency in SQL, Python, or R
  • Knowledge of data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI)
  • Understanding of healthcare systems and medical terminology
  • Statistical analysis and predictive modeling
  • Strong communication and presentation skills
  • Attention to detail and problem-solving abilities

Educational Requirements

A bachelor’s degree in Health Informatics, Statistics, Public Health, or a related field is required. A master’s degree or certification in healthcare analytics is advantageous.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Analyzing healthcare data to improve operations and outcomes
  • Creating dashboards and reports for stakeholders
  • Ensuring data accuracy and integrity
  • Collaborating with healthcare providers and administrators
  • Monitoring trends in patient care and resource use
  • Maintaining compliance with healthcare regulations

Average Salary

₹4,00,000 to ₹10,00,000 per year (India, based on experience and organization).
