Food Safety Specialist

Job Description

A Food Safety Specialist ensures that food products meet safety and quality standards. They inspect food processing facilities, analyze procedures, and enforce regulations to protect public health and prevent contamination.

Skills Required

  • Knowledge of food safety regulations and standards (e.g., HACCP, FSSAI)
  • Attention to detail and analytical skills
  • Ability to conduct audits and inspections
  • Strong communication and documentation skills
  • Problem-solving and decision-making abilities
  • Understanding of microbiology and food science

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in Food Science, Microbiology, or a related field is required. Certifications in food safety standards and auditing may be preferred.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Conducting inspections of food production and processing sites
  • Monitoring hygiene and safety practices
  • Testing food samples for contaminants
  • Ensuring compliance with food safety laws and guidelines
  • Training staff on safety protocols
  • Maintaining detailed records and reports

Average Salary

₹3,50,000 to ₹8,00,000 per year (India, varies by industry and experience).

Companies Offering This Role

  • Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI)
  • Nestlé India
  • ITC Limited
  • Hindustan Unilever
  • PepsiCo India
  • Quality control laboratories and food processing companies
