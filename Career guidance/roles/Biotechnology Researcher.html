Biotechnology Researcher

Job Description

A Biotechnology Researcher conducts scientific experiments and research to develop new products and processes in areas such as healthcare, agriculture, and environmental protection. Their work combines biology, chemistry, and technology to solve real-world problems.

Skills Required

  • Strong foundation in molecular biology, genetics, and biochemistry
  • Laboratory research and data analysis skills
  • Familiarity with bioinformatics tools and software
  • Problem-solving and critical thinking
  • Ability to work independently and in teams
  • Communication and technical writing skills

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in Biotechnology, Biology, or a related field is required. A master's degree or Ph.D. is preferred for advanced research roles and academic positions.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Designing and conducting experiments to test hypotheses
  • Developing new biotechnological products and processes
  • Analyzing data and documenting findings
  • Collaborating with other scientists and industry professionals
  • Staying current with scientific literature and advancements
  • Ensuring compliance with safety and ethical standards

Average Salary

₹5,00,000 to ₹12,00,000 per year (India, varies with experience and specialization).

Companies Offering This Role

  • Biocon
  • Dr. Reddy's Laboratories
  • Serum Institute of India
  • CSIR Laboratories
  • Indian Institute of Science (IISc)
  • Private biotech and pharmaceutical companies
