Climate Change Analyst

Job Description

A Climate Change Analyst studies the effects of climate change on the environment and society. They analyze data and trends to provide insights and recommendations for policy and action.

Skills Required

  • Strong analytical and research skills
  • Knowledge of environmental science and climate policies
  • Excellent communication and presentation abilities
  • Proficiency in data analysis software
  • Problem-solving and critical thinking skills

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in Environmental Science, Climate Studies, or a related field is typically required. Advanced degrees may be preferred for higher-level positions.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Conducting research on climate change impacts
  • Analyzing data and preparing reports
  • Advising organizations on climate strategies
  • Collaborating with government and NGOs on climate initiatives

Average Salary

₹4,00,000 to ₹10,00,000 per year (India, based on experience and location).

Companies Offering This Role

  • World Wildlife Fund (WWF)
  • Greenpeace
  • Indian Meteorological Department
  • Environmental NGOs
  • Government agencies focused on climate policy
