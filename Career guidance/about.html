User Guide

Welcome to our Career Guidance Platform! Follow these simple yet effective steps to make the most out of the services we offer and pave the way to a successful career.

Step 1

Begin by creating your personal account on our platform. If you already have an account, simply log in using your credentials. This will give you access to personalized features and save your progress securely.

Step 2

Fill out your profile by providing essential information such as your basic details (name, contact info), academic background, and areas of interest. The more detailed your profile, the better the platform can tailor recommendations specifically to your needs.

Step 3

Participate in our comprehensive career assessment quiz designed to evaluate your strengths, skills, and preferences. This quiz helps the system understand your unique abilities and inclinations, forming the basis for customized career suggestions.

Step 4

Based on the results of your quiz and profile data, you will receive curated career options that match your interests and skill set. Take your time to explore these suggestions and learn about various career paths that you may not have considered before.

Step 5

To support your chosen career path, the platform offers a selection of recommended courses, skill-building activities, and valuable resources. These tools are intended to help you enhance your qualifications and prepare effectively for your future profession.
