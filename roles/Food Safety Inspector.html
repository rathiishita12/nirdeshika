Food Safety Inspector

Job Description

A Food Safety Inspector ensures that food products meet safety and quality standards. They inspect food processing plants, restaurants, and other facilities to enforce hygiene regulations and prevent foodborne illnesses.

Skills Required

  • Thorough understanding of food safety laws and regulations
  • Attention to detail and observational skills
  • Strong analytical and documentation skills
  • Knowledge of laboratory testing and food sampling
  • Communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to handle field inspections and write detailed reports

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in Food Science, Microbiology, Public Health, or a related field is required. Additional certifications in food safety standards like HACCP or FSSAI licensing can be beneficial.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Inspecting food production facilities for compliance
  • Collecting samples for lab analysis
  • Enforcing hygiene practices and sanitation protocols
  • Identifying violations and issuing corrective actions
  • Training staff on food safety practices
  • Preparing inspection reports and documentation

Average Salary

₹3,00,000 to ₹7,00,000 per year (India, depending on experience and employer).

Organizations Offering This Role

  • Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI)
  • State Food and Drug Administrations
  • Quality assurance departments in food companies
  • Public health organizations
  • Food inspection agencies and consultancy firms
