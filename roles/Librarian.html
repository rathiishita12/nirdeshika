Librarian

Job Description

A Librarian manages the collection, organization, and accessibility of information resources in libraries. They assist users in finding information, manage library databases, and promote literacy and learning through various programs.

Skills Required

  • Strong organizational and cataloging skills
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities
  • Research and information retrieval expertise
  • Knowledge of digital library systems and databases
  • Customer service orientation
  • Attention to detail

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in Library Science or Information Science is typically required. A master's degree in Library and Information Science (MLIS) is preferred for advanced roles.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Organizing and managing library materials and databases
  • Helping patrons locate and use information resources
  • Maintaining digital records and cataloging systems
  • Conducting library programs and literacy initiatives
  • Acquiring new resources and managing library budgets
  • Staying updated with library technology and practices

Average Salary

₹2,50,000 to ₹6,00,000 per year (India, varies by institution and role).

Organizations Offering This Role

  • National Library of India
  • School and college libraries
  • University and research institutions
  • Government libraries and archives
  • Private educational institutions
  • Digital library service providers
