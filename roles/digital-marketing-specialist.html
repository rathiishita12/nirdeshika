Digital Marketing Specialist

Job Description

A Digital Marketing Specialist develops, implements, and manages marketing campaigns that promote a company and its products or services online. They play a major role in enhancing brand awareness within the digital space.

Skills Required

  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Search Engine Marketing (SEM)
  • Content creation and strategy
  • Social media management and advertising
  • Email marketing and automation tools
  • Data analysis and performance tracking
  • Familiarity with tools like Google Analytics, AdWords, and CRM systems

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, Business, or a related field is commonly required. Certifications in digital marketing (e.g., Google, HubSpot, Meta) are highly valued.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Developing and executing digital marketing strategies
  • Managing SEO/SEM and social media campaigns
  • Creating engaging content for blogs, websites, and social platforms
  • Analyzing campaign performance and optimizing for ROI
  • Coordinating with designers, developers, and marketing teams
  • Staying up-to-date with digital marketing trends and tools

Average Salary

₹4,00,000 to ₹10,00,000 per year (India, based on experience and organization).

Companies Offering This Role

  • Webchutney
  • Interactive Avenues
  • Accenture Digital
  • Google India
  • WATConsult
  • Startups and E-commerce platforms
