Renewable Energy Engineer

Job Description

Renewable Energy Engineers design and implement clean energy systems such as solar, wind, hydro, and bioenergy. They work to optimize energy efficiency, reduce emissions, and support the transition to sustainable energy sources.

Skills Required

  • Strong knowledge of renewable energy technologies
  • Electrical and mechanical engineering skills
  • Energy modeling and system design
  • Project management and teamwork
  • Understanding of environmental policies
  • Data analysis and simulation software proficiency

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's or master’s degree in Renewable Energy Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or a related discipline is required. Certifications in energy auditing or green building may be beneficial.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Designing and developing renewable energy systems
  • Conducting feasibility studies and site assessments
  • Monitoring and evaluating energy performance
  • Ensuring compliance with environmental and safety standards
  • Collaborating with architects, engineers, and stakeholders
  • Researching and applying innovative energy solutions

Average Salary

₹6,00,000 to ₹15,00,000 per year (India, depending on experience and sector).
