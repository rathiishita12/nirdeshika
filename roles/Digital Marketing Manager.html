Digital Marketing Manager

Job Description

A Digital Marketing Manager is responsible for developing, implementing, and managing marketing campaigns that promote a company's brand, products, and services. They play a major role in enhancing brand awareness and driving website traffic.

Skills Required

  • Strong knowledge of SEO, SEM, and web analytics tools
  • Expertise in content marketing and social media strategy
  • Google Ads and email marketing proficiency
  • Analytical and data-driven decision-making skills
  • Creativity and problem-solving ability
  • Leadership and project management capabilities

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, Business, or a related field is generally required. Certifications in digital marketing platforms (e.g., Google, HubSpot) are highly desirable.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Developing and executing digital marketing strategies
  • Overseeing social media and content marketing efforts
  • Managing online advertising campaigns
  • Monitoring and analyzing marketing analytics and KPIs
  • Collaborating with content creators, designers, and other teams
  • Staying up-to-date with digital marketing trends and tools

Average Salary

₹6,00,000 to ₹15,00,000 per year (India, depending on experience and industry).

Organizations Offering This Role

  • Google India
  • Flipkart
  • Amazon
  • Zoho
  • Infosys
  • Digital marketing agencies and e-commerce firms
