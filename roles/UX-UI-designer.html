UX/UI Designer

Job Description

A UX/UI Designer is responsible for creating user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing digital interfaces. They focus on enhancing the user experience through intuitive design, layout, and functionality in web and mobile applications.

Skills Required

  • Proficiency in design tools like Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, or InVision
  • Understanding of user-centered design principles
  • Wireframing and prototyping
  • Strong visual design and typography skills
  • Basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript is a plus
  • Empathy for users and attention to detail

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in Design, Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Computer Science, or a related field is typically required. Design certifications and portfolios demonstrating UX/UI skills are highly valued.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Conducting user research and analyzing user feedback
  • Creating wireframes, mockups, and prototypes
  • Collaborating with developers and stakeholders
  • Improving design based on user interaction data
  • Maintaining consistency in UI design across platforms
  • Staying up to date with industry trends and best practices

Average Salary

₹5,00,000 to ₹12,00,000 per year (India, depending on experience and company size).
