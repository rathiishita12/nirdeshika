Logistics Coordinator

Job Description

A Logistics Coordinator oversees the supply chain operations of a company. They manage the flow of goods, ensure timely delivery, and coordinate between suppliers, transportation companies, and customers.

Skills Required

  • Strong organizational and planning abilities
  • Excellent communication and negotiation skills
  • Knowledge of logistics and supply chain management
  • Problem-solving and analytical thinking
  • Proficiency in logistics software and Microsoft Excel
  • Attention to detail and multitasking capability

Educational Requirements

A bachelor's degree in Logistics, Supply Chain Management, Business Administration, or a related field is typically required. Internships or experience in logistics roles are advantageous.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Coordinating and monitoring supply chain operations
  • Liaising with suppliers, manufacturers, retailers, and customers
  • Tracking shipments and ensuring timely delivery
  • Managing inventory and maintaining accurate records
  • Addressing logistical problems and implementing solutions
  • Preparing reports and documentation related to logistics

Average Salary

₹3,00,000 to ₹7,00,000 per year (India, varies with experience and company size).
