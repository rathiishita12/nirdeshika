Data Scientist

Job Description

A Data Scientist collects, analyzes, and interprets complex data to help organizations make informed business decisions. They use statistical methods, machine learning, and data visualization tools to identify patterns and trends.

Skills Required

  • Programming in Python, R, or SQL
  • Statistical analysis and machine learning
  • Data cleaning and preprocessing
  • Data visualization with tools like Tableau or Matplotlib
  • Big data tools (e.g., Hadoop, Spark)
  • Critical thinking and business acumen

Educational Requirements

A bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, or related fields is a starting point. A Master’s or Ph.D. in Data Science or related disciplines is often preferred for advanced roles.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Gathering and interpreting data from various sources
  • Building predictive models and machine learning algorithms
  • Visualizing data insights for stakeholders
  • Collaborating with engineering and business teams
  • Conducting A/B testing and statistical analysis
  • Communicating findings to guide business decisions

Average Salary

₹6,00,000 to ₹20,00,000 per year (India, depending on skills and experience).
