Social Worker

Job Description

Social Workers support individuals, families, and communities by helping them cope with various life challenges. They work in schools, hospitals, mental health clinics, and government agencies to promote well-being and social functioning.

Skills Required

  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Empathy and emotional intelligence
  • Problem-solving and critical thinking abilities
  • Organizational and case management skills
  • Understanding of human behavior and social environments
  • Knowledge of relevant laws and ethical practices

Educational Requirements

A Bachelor of Social Work (BSW) is the minimum requirement. A Master of Social Work (MSW) is preferred for advanced roles. Additional certification or licensure may be required depending on the country or state.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Assessing clients' needs and developing intervention plans
  • Connecting clients with appropriate resources and services
  • Advocating for client rights and social justice
  • Providing counseling and crisis intervention
  • Maintaining case records and documentation
  • Collaborating with other professionals and agencies

Average Salary

₹2,50,000 to ₹6,00,000 per year (India, varies with specialization and experience).

Companies Offering This Role

  • UNICEF
  • CRY (Child Rights and You)
  • Save the Children India
  • TISS (Tata Institute of Social Sciences)
  • State and Central Government Departments
  • Various NGOs and mental health organizations
