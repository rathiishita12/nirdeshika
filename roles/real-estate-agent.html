Real Estate Agent

Job Description

A Real Estate Agent helps clients buy, sell, and rent properties. They act as intermediaries between buyers and sellers, guiding clients through the entire process and negotiating deals to achieve favorable outcomes.

Skills Required

  • Strong negotiation and sales skills
  • Knowledge of real estate laws and local market trends
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Marketing and property listing skills
  • Time management and organizational abilities
  • Customer service orientation

Educational Requirements

A high school diploma is the minimum requirement. However, a bachelor's degree in Business, Real Estate, or a related field is beneficial. Licensing through a recognized authority is mandatory in most regions.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Assisting clients in buying, selling, or renting properties
  • Conducting property viewings and open houses
  • Preparing and reviewing documents such as contracts
  • Providing market analysis and property evaluations
  • Negotiating offers between buyers and sellers
  • Staying updated with real estate laws and trends

Average Salary

₹3,00,000 to ₹12,00,000 per year (India, varies with commission and region).
