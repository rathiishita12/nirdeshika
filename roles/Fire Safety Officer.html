Fire Safety Officer

Job Description

A Fire Safety Officer is responsible for ensuring fire safety protocols are followed within buildings and workplaces. They conduct inspections, develop fire safety plans, provide training, and ensure compliance with fire codes and regulations.

Skills Required

  • Thorough understanding of fire codes and safety regulations
  • Strong analytical and risk assessment abilities
  • Excellent communication and leadership skills
  • Emergency response planning and execution
  • Attention to detail and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to train and educate staff

Educational Requirements

A diploma or degree in Fire Safety, Engineering, or Occupational Health & Safety is typically required. Certifications from fire safety institutions or firefighting training centers are highly valued.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Conducting fire risk assessments and inspections
  • Creating and implementing fire safety protocols
  • Training staff on fire prevention and evacuation procedures
  • Ensuring compliance with safety codes and standards
  • Investigating fire incidents and preparing reports
  • Liaising with fire departments and emergency services

Average Salary

₹3,50,000 to ₹8,00,000 per year (India, depending on experience and sector).
